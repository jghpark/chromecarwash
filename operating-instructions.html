Chrome Carwash

Operating Instructions

Follow these simple car wash operating instructions to make your vehicle shine!


Vehicle Vacuum

Turn to vac and vacuum rugs & upholstery.

Tire & Engine Cleaner

Turn to tire & engine cleaner and spray tires and engine.

Pre-soak

Turn to pre-soak and soak vehicle thoroughly.

Soap

Turn to soap and wash vehicle thoroughly.

Foaming Brush

Check brush for cleanliness first. Then turn to brush and brush vehicle thoroughly.

Rinse

Turn to rinse and rinse vehicle thoroughly.

Wax

Turn to wax and spray evening. Then turn to rinse entire vehicle thoroughly.

Spot-Free Rinse

For a spot free rinse, use this setting. After rinse, turn to spot-free rinse and rinse entire vehicle thoroughly.

Blow Dryer

Use dryer for key and door openings to prevent freezing in our freezing Alberta winter months. A car wash built for Albertans!
